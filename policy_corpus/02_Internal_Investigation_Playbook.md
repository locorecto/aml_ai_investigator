# Internal AML Investigation Playbook

## Objective
Provide a repeatable investigative workflow to ensure consistent, high-quality AML investigations.

## Step 1: Alert Review
- Identify triggering models and scenarios
- Review alert severity and risk score
- Confirm alert window and scope

## Step 2: Customer Context
- Review KYC profile and risk rating
- Compare activity against expected behavior
- Identify recent profile changes

## Step 3: Transaction Analysis
- Analyze transaction timeline and patterns
- Identify spikes, velocity changes, or anomalies
- Review counterparties and geographies

## Step 4: Network & Relationship Review
- Identify shared counterparties, accounts, or devices
- Assess potential layering or pass-through behavior

## Step 5: Hypothesis Testing
- List plausible benign explanations
- Identify missing information needed to confirm or refute

## Step 6: Decision & Documentation
- Determine SAR vs. No-SAR vs. Continue Investigation
- Document rationale with evidence references
- Draft SAR narrative if applicable

## Escalation Guidelines
- Escalate high-risk or complex cases to senior investigators
- Involve legal/compliance when uncertainty remains

## Investigator Notes
- Maintain clear, concise notes
- Avoid speculation unsupported by evidence
